About

This book will show you how to get a great sound in church for your musicians and speakers. With content one many areas of sound including explainations of how and why sound works and interacts.

Aims

To provide a simple guide for those doing sound in church. Specifically for those who are not sound engineers and get you the best sound you can in your church. To provide a fundemental understanding of how the phyics of sound, acoustics and electronics are used to create sound, and how we can maniuplate them to get great sounding mixes in church.

Why?

Sound engineering is one of the most techinical jobs in church. Unfortunatly most people are volunteers who are not sound engineers. Without knowing what you are doing, it is easy to create problems and bad sound.

Who's it for?

Volunteers doing sound, pastors, muscians, worship leaders, visual guys, anyone who might need to do something at the mixing desk.

Includes

This book covers all you will need to know to start having great sound in church.

  • Equipment

    The equipment used when doing sound engineering and explanations of how they work.

  • Set Up

    How to set up the equipment properly to ensure you get the best sound that you can.

  • Mixing

    How to use the mixing desk properly to get a great sounding mix.

  • Advanced

    Extra infomation about more advanced topics and tools that are used in sound engineering.

  • Tips

    Many short helpful tips that will take your mixes up to the next level.
