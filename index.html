About

This book will show you how to get a great sound in church for your music and speakers. With content on many areas of sound including explainations of how and why sound works and interacts.

Aims

To provide a simple guide for those doing sound in church. Specifically for those who are not sound engineers.

To provide a fundemental understanding of how phyics, acoustics and electronics are used to create sound, and how we can maniuplate them to get great sounding mixes in church.

To get you the best sound you can in your church.

Why?

Sound engineering is one of the most techinical jobs in church. Churches run on all so essential volunteers. Unfortunatly most volunteers are not sound engineers. Without knowing what you are doing, it is easy to create problems and bad sound.

Who's it for?

Volunteers doing sound, pastors, muscians, worship leaders, visual guys, anyone who might need to do something at the mixing desk or with any of the other sound equipment.

Written in two sections to make for simple reading. The first section containing all the need to know information, the second section runs parallel providing all the explainations behind it. This second section is "optional" and can be skipped over, it is for those curious types who want to dive deeper into the all physics of sound and get a greater understanding of it all.

Includes

This book covers all you will need to know to start having great sound in church.

  • Equipment

    The equipment used when doing sound engineering and explanations of how they work. Instruments, microphones, speakers, sound desks, amplfiers, etc.

  • Set Up

    How to set up the equipment properly to ensure you get the best sound that you can. Speaker placement, sound desk placement, how to correctly use microphones for each instrument, connecting it all together, etc.

  • Mixing

    How to use the mixing desk properly to get a great sounding mix. We will go over exactly how it works and what every button on the desk does. Then how we can use those buttons to control and shape the music to make it sound awesome!

  • Advanced

    Extra infomation about more advanced topics and tools that are used in sound engineering. Outboard gear and effects, compressors, reverbs, delays, digital desks, etc.

  • Tips

    Many short helpful tips that you can implement straight away and will immediately take your mixes up to the next level.
